Course Curriculum

01
BIM Fundamentals
Core concepts, workflows, and industry standards underpinning Building Information Modeling.
02
Architectural Modelling
Walls, floors, roofs, ceilings, stairs, and complete building envelope creation.

Structural Modelling
Columns, beams, foundations, and structural framing within the BIM environment.

MEP Systems
Mechanical, Electrical, and Plumbing modelling for coordinated building services.

Families & Parametric Components
Creating and customising intelligent families and parametric Revit components.

Automated material schedules, door/window schedules, and QTO reports.

Coordination Basics
Clash detection concepts and inter-discipline model coordination workflows.

High-quality renders, walkthroughs, and visual presentations for client delivery.
